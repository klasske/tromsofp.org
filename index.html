About TromsøFP

Interested in functional programming (e.g. Clojure, Erlang, F#, Haskell, Lisp...)? Perfect, come join us!

We are a group of functional programming enthusiasts living in and around Tromsø, who enjoy a friendly discussion about programming languages and coding philosophy. The group spans the full spectrum from the die-hard Haskeller to those who have hardly heard of functional programming, but are curious to learn more.

Our aim is to teach and learn from each other, exchange ideas and have fun. We value hands on coding sessions more than lectures, and think pair and group programming is a blast.
